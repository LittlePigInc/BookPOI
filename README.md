# BookPOI -- Literary Places & Notes

## Short Description

Discover real-world locations from your favorite books. Create map-based
notes and explore literature around you.

## Full Description

**BookPOI connects the stories you love with real places.** Create
personal, map-based points of interest (POIs) linked to books, scenes,
or characters. Add your own notes or short excerpts (limited in length
to protect copyright) to remember what makes each location meaningful.

When traveling, BookPOI can notify you when you're near one of your
saved literary spots.

### Features

-   Save books to your personal collection
-   Create location-based POIs tied to specific books or scenes
-   Add personal notes or short text excerpts (character-limited)
-   Receive optional location notifications when near saved POIs
-   Browse and revisit your literary locations on an interactive map
