# Privacy Policy for BookPOI

*Last updated: 2025-11-19*

BookPOI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy
explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## Information We Collect

### 1. User-Provided Data

-   Notes, POIs, and short text excerpts you create\
-   Optional account or profile information (if applicable)

### 2. Device & App Data

-   App usage statistics\
-   Crash logs\
-   Device information (model, OS version)

### 3. Location Data (Optional)

Location is only used to: - Show nearby POIs - Send optional proximity
notifications

Your precise location is **never stored on our servers**.

## How We Use Your Information

-   To provide core features (maps, POIs, notifications)
-   To improve app stability and performance
-   To moderate user-generated content
-   To respond to support or removal requests

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell or share your data with third parties except: -
Service providers that help operate the app (e.g., analytics, crash
logs)

## User-Generated Content

Content you upload may be visible to other users if shared publicly.\
All content is subject to moderation to ensure compliance with our UGC
Policy.

## Your Rights

You may request: - Removal of your data - Removal of inappropriate or
copyrighted content posted by others

Contact: **support@bookpoi.online**

## Children's Privacy

BookPOI is not intended for children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Updates will be posted
here.
