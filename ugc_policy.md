# BookPOI User-Generated Content (UGC) Policy

## Overview

BookPOI allows users to create and share map-based notes, locations, and
brief text excerpts. This policy ensures that user-generated content
remains safe, legal, and respectful.

## Allowed Content

-   Personal notes\
-   Public-domain text\
-   Short excerpts within character limits\
-   Locations relevant to books or literary themes

## Prohibited Content

-   Copyrighted text beyond short excerpts\
-   Full paragraphs, chapters, or large quotations\
-   Hate speech, harassment, or inappropriate content\
-   Spam or misleading information\
-   Locations intended for stalking or harmful behavior

## Moderation Process

-   All users can report content directly in the app\
-   Reported content is manually reviewed\
-   Violating content is removed promptly\
-   Repeat offenders may lose posting privileges

## How to Report Content

Inside the app: 1. Open the menu (top-left) 2. Tap **Report Content or
Request Removal** 3. Follow the instructions or contact support

## Support Contact

**support@bookpoi.online**

We respond to all removal or moderation requests quickly.
